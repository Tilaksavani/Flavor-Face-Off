FLAVOR Face-off ” - Cooking and Baking Competition for Female Entrepreneur

FLAVOR Face-off ” - Cooking and Baking Competition for Female Entrepreneur is a competition for female staff (all cadre) and female students of CHARUSAT. The primary objective of this event is to prepare Novel dish in the competition and sell them and foster a sense of unity, gratitude, inclusiveness and show female entrepreneur enthusiasm withinthe CHARUSAT community. After the competition, the dishes prepared by the female students will be sell by them selves and show their amount collection based on their dishes.
